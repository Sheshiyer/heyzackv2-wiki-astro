## The 5-Minute Overview

### 1. What Is HeyZack? (1 min)

AI-powered smart home and building automation with 128 products across 15 categories. Dual market: 50% B2C (homeowners), 50% B2B (commercial buildings).

**Brand Promise**: "AI Automation for Residential & Commercial Buildings"

### 2. Who Are We Talking To? (2 min)

**B2C Audiences:**
- [Tech-Savvy Homeowners](../audience/primary-persona.md) — Early adopters wanting intelligent automation
- [Energy-Conscious Consumers](../audience/secondary-personas.md) — Cost and sustainability focused
- [Safety-Focused Families](../audience/secondary-personas.md) — Security and peace of mind

**B2B Audiences:**
- [Property Managers](../audience/b2b-personas.md) — Multi-property portfolio efficiency
- [Hospitality Operators](../audience/b2b-personas.md) — Guest experience differentiation

### 3. How Do We Sound? (1 min)

| Attribute | We Are | We Are Not |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Tone** | Friendly, confident, helpful | Robotic, pushy, overly casual |
| **Language** | Plain English, benefit-focused | Technical jargon, feature-dumping |
| **Voice** | Knowledgeable guide | Salesperson or tech manual |

See [Voice & Tone Guide](../brand/voice-tone.md) for examples.
