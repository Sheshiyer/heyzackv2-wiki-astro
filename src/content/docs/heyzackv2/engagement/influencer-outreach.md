# Influencer Outreach

Ready-to-use DM scripts and email templates for recruiting influencers and thought leaders.

---

## Outreach Strategy Overview

| Segment | Platform | Voice | Primary Goal |
|---------|----------|-------|--------------|
| B2B Property Managers | LinkedIn, Email | The Helpful Peer | Partnership + Case Studies |
| B2B Hospitality | LinkedIn, Email | The Helpful Peer | Beta Testing + ROI Proof |
| B2C Tech Reviewers | YouTube, Instagram | The Knowledgeable Friend | Honest Reviews |

## B2B LinkedIn DM Templates

### Template 1: The Peer Recognition Opener

```
Hi [Name],

I just read your piece on [specific pain point, e.g., "BMS vendor lock-in"]. 
You nailed the frustration every property manager faces.

I'm [Your Name] from HeyZack. We've built an AI-powered building automation 
platform: portfolio-scale control at 95% lower cost than traditional BMS. 
No service contracts. No vendor lock-in.

We're launching on Kickstarter and partnering with industry operators.

Here's what we're offering:
→ Early platform access (test across your properties)
→ 20% affiliate commission on referrals
→ Co-branded case study showcasing your results

Would you be open to a 15-minute call?

— [Your Name], HeyZack
```

### Template 2: The Data-Driven Proof Angle

```
Hi [Name],

Quick question: If you could cut your portfolio's operating costs by 20-30% 
without adding headcount or signing vendor contracts, would you want the data?

Our AI automation platform delivered 28% energy savings for a 30-building 
portfolio in Q3—all DIY-installed, no $85K deployment fees.

I'd love to send you:
→ The 30-building case study (documented ROI)
→ Technical comparison vs. Honeywell/Johnson Controls
→ Early backer pricing (exclusive to industry partners)

20-minute call?

— [Your Name], HeyZack
```

### Template 3: The Vendor Liberation Story

```
Hi [Name],

You've been vocal about vendor lock-in. I respect that—every operator feels it.

HeyZack was built to solve that problem. One dashboard for unlimited properties. 
DIY installation. Open protocols (Zigbee, WiFi, Matter). No traps.

We're looking for 10-15 industry partners who want to prove there's a better model.

Partnership details:
→ Test units for 3-5 buildings
→ Co-branded webinar opportunity
→ 20% ongoing affiliate revenue
→ White-label case study (we document, you approve)

Are you in? Let's schedule 15 minutes.

— [Your Name], HeyZack
```

## B2C Instagram/YouTube DM Templates

### Template 1: The Value-First Approach

```
Hey [Name]! 👋

Love your content on [specific video topic]. Your take on [specific point] 
was spot-on.

We're launching HeyZack—an AI-powered smart home system that solves the 
fragmentation nightmare (one app, no subscriptions, actually learns your habits).

Here's what we'd offer:
→ Free review kit (starter bundle, $1,200 value)
→ Early access before public launch
→ Affiliate link (15% commission on sales)
→ Behind-the-scenes founder access for Q&A

No strings—if it sucks, say so. We'd rather honest feedback than fake praise.

Want me to send specs?

— [Name], HeyZack Team
```

### Template 2: The Tech Reviewer Pitch

```
Hey [Name],

Quick pitch: We built HeyZack because we got tired of the smart home lie.

The pitch: One app. No subscriptions. AI that actually learns (not just schedules). 
$800-$2,000 for a whole-home setup. DIY install in 30 minutes.

The catch: None. We make money on hardware, not data.

We're launching on Kickstarter and looking for 5 tech reviewers who'll 
give us honest coverage—good or bad.

Interested in a review kit?

— [Name], HeyZack
```

→ 90-day trial with full support

Worth a conversation?
