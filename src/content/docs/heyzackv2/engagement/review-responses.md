# Review Response Templates

Ready-to-use templates for responding to customer reviews across all platforms.

---

## Response Strategy by Rating

| Rating | Strategy | Tone |
|--------|----------|------|
| 5 Stars | Gratitude + community invite | Celebratory |
| 4 Stars | Thank + address concern + invite feedback | Constructive |
| 3 Stars | Acknowledge + problem-solve + follow up | Empathetic |
| 2 Stars | Empathy first + resolution + take private | Apologetic |
| 1 Star | Immediate escalation + private resolution | Crisis mode |

---

## 5-Star Review Responses

Keep brief (2-3 sentences). Express gratitude, reinforce value, invite community.

### Template 5.1: Energy Savings Callback (B2C)

**Review:** "My energy bill dropped $40 in the first month. Can't believe how easy this was to set up."

**Response:**
```
$40 in month one—that's exactly what we designed HeyZack to do! 🎉 
Thank you for sharing. If you ever want to swap automation ideas, 
our Discord community loves hearing real-world setups like yours.
```

### Template 5.2: Simplicity Appreciation (B2C)

**Review:** "Finally, a smart home system my wife actually uses. One app, everything works."

**Response:**
```
"My wife actually uses it" might be the best compliment we've ever received! 
Thanks for the kind words. We built HeyZack specifically to eliminate the 
complexity that makes smart homes frustrating. Enjoy!
```

### Template 5.3: Property Manager Success (B2B)

**Review:** "Cut operating costs by 25% across 12 properties. Payback in 4 months."

**Response:**
```
25% across 12 properties is outstanding ROI! Thank you for trusting HeyZack 
with your portfolio. If you'd ever like to collaborate on a case study to 
help other property managers, we'd love to chat.
```

## 4-Star Review Responses

Thank them, address the missing star, invite further feedback.

### Template 4.1: "Almost Perfect" Response

**Review:** "Great system, easy to set up. Wish the app had a dark mode."

**Response:**
```
Thank you for the feedback! Dark mode is actually in development—you're 
not the first to ask. 😄 We'll ping you when it launches. In the meantime, 
feel free to drop feature requests in our community Discord!
```

### Template 4.2: Feature Request Acknowledgment

**Review:** "Works well, but would love integration with [specific system]."

**Response:**
```
Really appreciate the honest feedback! [Integration] is on our roadmap—
we're prioritizing based on community demand. If you haven't already, 
vote for it in our feature request channel. Your voice matters!
```

## 3-Star Review Responses

Acknowledge concerns empathetically. Offer concrete solutions.

### Template 3.1: Setup Difficulty

**Review:** "Took longer than expected to set up. System works fine once running."

**Response:**
```
We're sorry the setup took longer than expected—that's not the experience 
we want. Could you share which step was tricky? We're constantly improving 
our setup guides and your feedback directly shapes that work. 

DM us or email support@heyzack.com—we'd love to make it right.
```

### Template 3.2: Connectivity Issues

**Review:** "Occasional disconnects. Good when it works but frustrating sometimes."

**Response:**
```
We hear you, and we're sorry for the frustration. Intermittent disconnects 
are usually fixable—often it's router placement or WiFi congestion. 

Our support team can diagnose remotely if you reach out: support@heyzack.com. 
We want this to work perfectly for you.
```

## 2-Star Review Responses

Lead with empathy. Take the conversation private. Commit to resolution.

### Template 2.1: Unmet Expectations

**Review:** "Not as smart as advertised. Still feels like I'm managing everything manually."

**Response:**
```
We're genuinely sorry HeyZack hasn't met your expectations—that's not 
what we want anyone to experience. The AI learning typically takes 1-2 weeks 
to build accurate patterns, but if it's not working for you, let's fix it.

Please email support@heyzack.com with your account details. We'll personally 
review your setup and make sure you're getting the intelligence you signed up for.
```

### Template 2.2: Support Frustration

**Review:** "Had issues, support was slow to respond. Eventually got help but frustrating."

**Response:**
```
We're sorry our support didn't meet your expectations—you deserved faster 
help. We're actively expanding our team to improve response times.

Glad your issue was eventually resolved, but we know "eventually" isn't 
good enough. Thank you for sticking with us, and please reach out directly 
to support@heyzack.com if anything else comes up—we'll prioritize you.
```

## 1-Star Review Responses

Crisis mode: Immediate, personal, empathetic. Move to private channel ASAP.

### Template 1.1: Complete Failure Response

**Review:** "Total waste of money. Nothing works. Returning immediately."

**Response:**
```
We're deeply sorry you've had this experience—this is absolutely not 
acceptable and not reflective of what HeyZack should be.

Please reach out immediately to support@heyzack.com or DM us directly. 
We want to either resolve this completely or ensure a full refund—whatever 
makes this right for you. Your experience matters to us.
```

### Template 1.2: Safety/Security Concern

**Review:** "Security system failed to alert me. Unacceptable for a safety product."

**Response:**
```
We take security concerns extremely seriously. We're deeply sorry this 
happened and understand your frustration completely.

Please contact us immediately at security@heyzack.com with your account 
details. We need to investigate this urgently and ensure your home is 
properly protected. A member of our leadership team will personally 
follow up within 24 hours.
```

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Amazon Reviews
- Respond within 24 hours
- Keep under 100 words
- Include direct support email

### Google Reviews
- Respond within 48 hours
- Include business name in response
- Reference specific feedback points

### Trustpilot
- Respond within 24 hours
- Tag as "Replied" for status visibility
- Use professional formatting

### App Store / Play Store
- Maximum 350 characters
- Link to support resources
- Acknowledge specific app issues
