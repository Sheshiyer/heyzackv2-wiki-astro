# HeyZack Messaging Framework

Master messaging document for consistent communication across all channels.

---

## Core Value Proposition

### Universal (B2B + B2C)

**One-Liner:**
> "AI-powered building automation that actually learns—one app, no subscriptions, real savings."

**Elevator Pitch (30 seconds):**
> "HeyZack is an AI-powered smart home and building automation system that eliminates the fragmentation nightmare. One app controls everything—climate, security, lighting, energy. No monthly subscriptions for core features. The AI actually learns your patterns instead of just running schedules. For homeowners, it means $30-70/month in energy savings. For property managers, it's 20-30% operating cost reduction across your entire portfolio."

## B2C Messaging

### Primary Value Proposition

**Headline:** Your Home, Intelligently Automated

**Subhead:** One app. No subscriptions. Actually learns your habits.

**Supporting Points:**
1. **Unified Control** — Stop juggling 7 different apps. Control everything from one place.
2. **Genuine AI** — Pattern recognition that learns your behavior, not just timers.
3. **Real Savings** — $30-70/month in energy savings, visible on your dashboard.
4. **No Subscription Tax** — Core features free forever. Optional cloud storage only.
5. **30-Minute Setup** — DIY installation. No electrician required.

### Proof Points

| Claim | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| Energy savings | "Average user saves $47/month on utilities" |
| Setup simplicity | "30-minute installation, no tools required" |
| AI learning | "Learns patterns within 2 weeks of normal use" |
| Compatibility | "Works with 128 device types across 15 categories" |
| No lock-in | "Open protocols: WiFi, Zigbee, Matter, Bluetooth" |

### Emotional Triggers

- **Relief:** "No more app hell"
- **Confidence:** "Finally understand where your energy goes"
- **Pride:** "The home everyone wants to see"
- **Peace of mind:** "Know your family is safe without constant checking"

## Objection Handling

### B2C Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I tried smart home before, it was frustrating" | "You're not crazy—smart homes HAVE been a mess. 7 apps, things that stop working after updates. HeyZack is what we built after that same frustration. One app, everything works together." |
| "I'm not technical enough" | "If you can use a smartphone, you can set up HeyZack. Scan QR codes, follow the app. 30 minutes, done. Your non-tech partner will actually like this." |
| "What about privacy?" | "Your data stays on your devices by default. Local storage, local control. Cloud is optional. We make money on hardware, not selling your data." |
| "Why no subscription?" | "We believe core home automation shouldn't be a monthly tax. We profit from hardware—you get features without ongoing fees." |
| "Will it work with my existing devices?" | "HeyZack supports WiFi, Zigbee, Matter, and Bluetooth. Most major brands work. Check our compatibility tool before you buy." |

### B2B Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too good to be true pricing" | "Here's a case study with documented ROI. 30-day money-back guarantee. Test it on one building first." |
| "DIY means unprofessional" | "DIY installation, commercial-grade specs. We offer SLA guarantees and enterprise support tiers." |
| "What if your company fails?" | "Local control means your system keeps working without our servers. Open protocols mean you can migrate. Your data, your choice." |
| "Our buildings are too old" | "We've retrofitted 1970s buildings. No new wiring required. Wireless sensors, existing HVAC integration." |
| "My team doesn't have time" | "2-hour onboarding per property. Centralized dashboard means less time managing, not more." |
