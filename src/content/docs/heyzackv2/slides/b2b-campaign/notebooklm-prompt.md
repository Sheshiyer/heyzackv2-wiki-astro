# NotebookLM Prompt: HeyZack B2B Campaign Deck

## Instructions for NotebookLM

Create a 15-slide sales enablement presentation for HeyZack, targeting commercial property managers and facilities directors. The presentation should be consultative, ROI-focused, and demonstrate deep understanding of property management challenges.

---

## Brand Styling Requirements

**Colors**:
- Primary Blue: #243984 — Headlines, trust elements
- Primary Pink: #E82F89 — Savings numbers, CTAs, highlights
- Background: White or light grey (#F8FAFC)
- Gradient: Blue to Pink for key moments

**Typography**:
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif, 48-64px
- Body: Regular sans-serif, 18-24px
- Savings/ROI numbers: Extra bold, 72-120px

**Visual Style**:
- Professional property management scenarios
- Dashboard screenshots
- Before/After comparisons
- ROI calculators and savings graphs
