# NotebookLM Prompt: HeyZack B2B Pitch Deck

## Instructions for NotebookLM

Create a 12-slide investor pitch presentation for HeyZack, an AI-powered building automation company. The presentation should be professional, data-driven, and compelling for venture capital investors and strategic partners.

---

## Brand Styling Requirements

**Colors** (use consistently throughout):
- Primary Blue: #243984 — Headlines, key data points, trust elements
- Primary Pink: #E82F89 — CTAs, accents, emphasis moments
- Gradient: Blue to Pink — Title slide, closing slide, key moments
- Background: Light grey (#F8FAFC) or white with subtle texture

**Typography**:
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif, 48-64px
- Body: Clean sans-serif, 18-24px
- Data/Stats: Extra bold, 72-120px for impact numbers

**Visual Style**:
- Clean, minimal, premium tech aesthetic
- Generous whitespace
- Icons: Outline style, 2px stroke
- Charts: Simple, brand-colored, large data labels

## Formatting Rules

1. **One message per slide** — Don't overload
2. **Headlines under 8 words** — Punchy and memorable
3. **Max 4 bullets per slide** — Respect attention spans
4. **Large numbers for data** — 72px+ for key stats
5. **Consistent colors** — Blue for trust, pink for action
6. **Whitespace** — At least 20% of each slide should be empty

---

## Tone & Voice

- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Data-driven** — Every claim has a number
- **Professional** — This is a business pitch, not a product demo
- **Urgent** — The market opportunity is now
