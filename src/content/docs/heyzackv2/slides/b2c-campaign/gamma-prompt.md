# Gamma.app Prompt: HeyZack B2C Crowdfunding Deck

## Presentation Brief

Create a **12-slide crowdfunding campaign deck** for HeyZack, an AI-powered smart home system launching on Kickstarter.

**Audience**: Tech-savvy homeowners, environmentally-conscious consumers, early adopters
**Tone**: Approachable, aspirational, exciting
**Style**: Lifestyle-focused, warm, modern tech

---

## Theme & Styling

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #243984 (deep blue) — trust, reliability
- Accent: #E82F89 (vibrant pink) — excitement, CTAs
- Background: White with lifestyle imagery
- Text: #2E2D2C (near black)

**Typography**:
- Headlines: Bold, friendly sans-serif
- Body: Warm, readable, conversational

**Visual Style**:
- Lifestyle photography (real homes, real people)
- Warm lighting, inviting spaces
- Product photos integrated naturally
- Emotional connection over technical specs
