# NotebookLM Prompt: HeyZack B2C Crowdfunding Deck

## Instructions for NotebookLM

Create a 12-slide crowdfunding campaign presentation for HeyZack, an AI-powered smart home system. The presentation should be warm, approachable, and emotionally compelling for consumer audiences.

---

## Brand Styling Requirements

**Colors** (use consistently throughout):
- Primary Blue: #243984 — Trust, reliability, security features
- Primary Pink: #E82F89 — Excitement, CTAs, emotion moments
- Gradient: Blue to Pink — Title slide, closing slide, key CTAs
- Background: White or light grey with lifestyle imagery

**Typography**:
- Headlines: Bold, friendly sans-serif, 48-64px
- Body: Warm, readable, 18-24px
- CTAs: Bold, uppercase, button styling

**Visual Style**:
- Lifestyle photography (real homes, real people)
- Warm, inviting lighting
- Products shown in natural settings
- Diverse, inclusive representation
- Emotional storytelling over technical specs

## Formatting Rules

1. **Lead with emotion** — Benefits before features
2. **Use real stories** — Tyler and Emma make it relatable
3. **Show, don't tell** — Lifestyle imagery over product specs
4. **Clear CTAs** — Every slide should have a next step implied
5. **Accessible language** — No jargon, no tech-speak

---

## Tone & Voice

- **Warm and Friendly** — Like a helpful neighbor
- **Aspirational** — The home you've always wanted
- **Trustworthy** — Real people, real savings
- **Exciting** — This is the future, and it's available now
