# NotebookLM Prompt: HeyZack Product Overview Deck

## Instructions for NotebookLM

Create a 10-slide product overview presentation for HeyZack, showcasing the complete smart home product ecosystem. The presentation should be clean, informative, and organized by product category.

---

## Brand Styling Requirements

**Colors** (use consistently):
- Primary Blue: #243984 — Category headers, trust elements
- Primary Pink: #E82F89 — Product counts, accents
- Gradient: Blue to Pink — Title and closing slides
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) or light grey (#F8FAFC)

**Typography**:
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif, 48-64px
- Body: Clean sans-serif, 18-24px
- Numbers: Extra bold for product counts

**Visual Style**:
- Clean product photography
- White backgrounds for product images
- Organized grids and layouts
- Category icons for quick scanning

## Formatting Rules

1. **One category per slide** — Clear organization
2. **Feature 4 products max per slide** — Don't overwhelm
3. **Consistent product grid layouts** — Professional appearance
4. **Key benefit callouts** — Why this category matters
5. **Clean backgrounds** — Let products shine

---

## Tone & Voice

- **Informative** — Focus on what products do
- **Professional** — Suitable for B2B audiences
- **Organized** — Easy to navigate
- **Comprehensive** — Show the full ecosystem
