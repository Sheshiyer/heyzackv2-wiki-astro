# Visual Direction Guide

Brand visual guidelines for HeyZack presentations, pitch decks, and marketing materials.

---

## Brand Colors

### Primary Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| HeyZack Blue | `#243984` | Primary brand, headers, CTAs |
| HeyZack Pink | `#E82F89` | Accent, highlights, energy |
| HeyZack Black | `#2E2D2C` | Text, backgrounds |

### Secondary Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Light Gray | `#F5F5F5` | Backgrounds, cards |
| Medium Gray | `#888888` | Secondary text |
| Success Green | `#22C55E` | Positive metrics, savings |
| Warning Amber | `#F59E0B` | Alerts, attention |

### Gradient

```css
/* Primary gradient */
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #243984 0%, #E82F89 100%);
```

## Typography

### Font Stack

- **Headlines:** Inter Bold / SF Pro Display Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular / SF Pro Text
- **Code/Data:** JetBrains Mono / SF Mono

### Type Scale

| Element | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| H1 (Hero) | 48-64px | Bold |
| H2 (Section) | 32-40px | Bold |
| H3 (Subsection) | 24-28px | Semibold |
| Body | 16-18px | Regular |
| Caption | 12-14px | Regular |

## Photography Style

### Do Use

[+] **Real Environments**
- Actual homes, not staged showrooms
- Diverse family compositions
- Natural lighting, lived-in spaces
- Real property management offices

[+] **Authentic Moments**
- People interacting with devices naturally
- Candid expressions, not posed smiles
- Work-from-home scenarios
- Multi-generational families

[+] **Tech Integration**
- Devices in context (not isolated product shots)
- App screens on real phones in real hands
- Dashboard views on laptops in office settings

### Don't Use

- Stock photo clichés
- All-white minimalist "smart home" rooms
- Overly posed family scenes
- Generic business handshakes
- Futuristic/sci-fi aesthetics

## Presentation Templates

### Slide Types

#### 1. Title Slide
- Full-bleed gradient background
- Logo top-left
- Headline centered
- Subhead below

#### 2. Problem Slide
- Dark background
- Pain point headline in white
- Supporting stats in pink accent

#### 3. Solution Slide
- Light background
- Product screenshot or diagram
- 3 key benefits as icons

#### 4. Data/ROI Slide
- Clean white background
- Large numbers in blue
- Comparison charts in brand colors

#### 5. Testimonial Slide
- Quote in large italic text
- Customer photo (if available)
- Name, title, company below

#### 6. CTA Slide
- Gradient background
- Single clear action
- Contact info / URL

## Icon Style

### Guidelines

- **Style:** Outlined, 2px stroke weight
- **Corners:** Rounded (4px radius)
- **Color:** Single color (blue or pink)
- **Size:** 24x24px base, scale as needed

### Icon Categories

| Category | Icons Needed |
|----------|--------------|
| Devices | Thermostat, Camera, Lock, Light, Sensor |
| Actions | Control, Automate, Monitor, Save |
| Benefits | Time, Money, Security, Comfort |
| Emotions | Relief, Pride, Peace, Confidence |

---

## Motion Guidelines

### Animation Principles

1. **Purposeful** — Motion should communicate, not decorate
2. **Quick** — 200-300ms for UI transitions
3. **Smooth** — Ease-out curves for entries, ease-in for exits
4. **Consistent** — Same timing across all interactions

### Video Style

- **Pace:** Energetic but not frantic
- **Music:** Upbeat electronic, not generic corporate
- **Text:** Bold, readable, on-screen for 2+ seconds
- **Transitions:** Clean cuts, minimal effects

| Twitter/X | Landscape | 1200×675 |

### Template Elements

- Logo watermark: Bottom-right, 10% opacity
- Text safe zone: 15% margin from edges
- CTA placement: Bottom third of frame
