# HeyZack 30-Day Pre-Launch Social Content Calendar

## Campaign Overview

**Launch Date:** T-30 to Launch Day  
**Content Mix:** 40% Educational | 30% Social Proof | 20% Product Features | 10% Entertainment  
**Voice Strategy:** Dual-track (B2B: Professional/Data-driven | B2C: Friendly/Conversational)

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Twitter/X
- Character limit: 280 (use threads for longer content)
- Best times: 8 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM EST
- Engagement: Respond within 1 hour, quote-tweet relevant conversations

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone, data-driven content
- Best times: 7:30 AM, 11 AM, 5 PM EST
- Engagement: Multi-post threads, lead magnets, industry insights

### Instagram
- Visual-first, lifestyle imagery
- Best times: 12 PM, 5 PM, 9 PM EST
- Use Stories for polls, behind-the-scenes, quick updates

### TikTok/Reels
- Entertainment-first, relatable content
- Best times: 7 PM, 9 PM EST
- Leverage trending sounds and formats

### Reddit
- Authentic, community-focused content
- Never overtly promotional
- Engage as a fellow community member
