## About HeyZack

**Brand Promise**: "AI Automation for Residential & Commercial Buildings"

HeyZack offers a comprehensive ecosystem of 128 smart devices across 15 categories, serving both residential homes (B2C) and commercial buildings (B2B) with a 50/50 market split.

### Key Differentiators

- **AI-Driven Automation** — True learning that adapts to behaviors, not just schedules
- **DIY Installation** — Complete setup in under 30 minutes, no tools required
- **Unified Control** — One app for everything, voice-enabled
- **Energy Efficiency** — 15-30% energy savings through intelligent management
- **Privacy-First** — Local storage options, edge computing, bank-level encryption
