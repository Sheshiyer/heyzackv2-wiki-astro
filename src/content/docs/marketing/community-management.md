# Community Management

Ready-to-use scripts for Discord server and Facebook Group management. Includes channel structure, welcome messages, daily prompts, and moderation templates. Aligned with the [HeyZack Brand Voice](../brand/voice-tone.md).

## Discord Server Setup

### Recommended Channel Structure

#### B2B Channels

```
📋 B2B PROPERTY MANAGERS
├── #pm-welcome (Rules, Getting Started)
├── #pm-portfolio-talk (Multi-property management)
├── #pm-roi-showcase (Energy savings data)
├── #pm-tech-questions (Installation, troubleshooting)
└── #pm-feature-requests (Vote on enterprise features)

🏨 B2B HOSPITALITY
├── #hospitality-welcome
├── #guest-experience (Automation ideas for hotels/Airbnb)
├── #hospitality-roi (Revenue impact discussions)

```

#### B2C Channels

```
B2C HOMEOWNERS
├── #home-welcome (Rules, Getting Started)
├── #setup-help (Installation questions)
├── #automation-ideas (Share your favorite automations)
├── #energy-savings (Post your bill reductions)
├── #show-off (Home setup photos/videos)
└── #smart-home-refugees (For those fleeing SmartThings/Nest)

🔧 TECHNICAL
├── #integrations (Nest, Ring, Philips Hue compatibility)
├── #feature-requests (Vote on upcoming features)
└── #beta-testing (Early access program)

📢 ANNOUNCEMENTS
├── #campaign-updates (Launch day, stretch goals, shipping)
├── #media-mentions (Press, reviews, YouTuber coverage)
└── #weekly-digest (Curated highlights)
```

### Server Pin Post (Rules)

```
— **COMMUNITY RULES & ETIQUETTE**

**The Golden Rules:**
1. **Be Helpful, Not Judgmental** – No "stupid questions" exist here
2. **Share Real Results** – Energy savings? Post your before/after bills
3. **Stay On-Topic** – Use the right channels (B2B in B2B, B2C in B2C)
4. **No Spam** – Promoting your own products = instant ban
5. **Respect Privacy** – Don't share others' personal info without consent

**Community Perks:**
• Early access to beta features
• Direct line to product team
• Exclusive backer-only stretch goals
• Monthly "Setup of the Month" spotlight ($100 HeyZack credit)

**Need Help?**
Ping @CommunityTeam or DM a moderator.

Let's build something amazing together. →
```

## Moderation Response Templates

### Scenario 1: Price Objection

**User says:** "This seems expensive for what it is."

**Response:**
```
Hey [Name]! Fair question—let's break it down:

**What You're Paying For:**
- Commercial-grade components (IP65 rated, -20°C to 60°C)
- Genuine AI learning (not just timers)
- No monthly subscriptions (competitors charge $5-15/mo per device)
- Bank-level encryption + local storage options

**Cost Comparison:**
- Nest setup (thermostat + 3 cameras + doorbell): ~$1,100 + $30/mo = $1,460 Year 1
- HeyZack equivalent: $780 total, $0/mo = $780 Year 1

Plus, average backer saves $47/month on energy = pays for itself in 16 months.

Does that help clarify?
```

---

### Scenario 2: Shipping Delay Concerns

**User says:** "How do I know you'll actually ship on time?"

**Response:**
```
Totally understand the skepticism—crowdfunding delays are infamous.

**What We've Done Differently:**
• Manufacturing partner locked in (producing for 8+ years)
• Prototypes already functional (not vaporware)
• Conservative timeline (Q2 2026 = buffer built in)
• Weekly production updates (you'll see EXACTLY where we're at)

**If We Miss Our Date:**
Partial refunds for every 30 days of delay (detailed in campaign FAQs).

Check out the 'Manufacturing Roadmap' section on the campaign page—we're 
serious about transparency. Questions?
```

### Scenario 3: Technical Compatibility Question

**User says:** "Will this work with my existing Nest thermostat?"

**Response:**
```
Great question! Short answer: **Yes.**

HeyZack works alongside your Nest via the unified app—you'll control both 
from one interface. The AI learns patterns from both systems.

**Pro Tip:** If you ever want to replace the Nest, HeyZack thermostats 
integrate natively and cost 60% less. But zero pressure—we're designed 
to play nice with what you already own.

Full compatibility list: [Link]

Running another device brand? Let me know—I'll confirm!
```

---

### Scenario 4: Privacy/Security Concern

**User says:** "How do I know my camera footage isn't being sold?"

**Response:**
```
**We're glad you asked.** Privacy is non-negotiable for us.

**Here's Our Policy (Plain English):**
1. **Local Storage Default:** Cameras record to SD cards—footage never 
   touches our servers unless YOU enable cloud backup
2. **You Own Your Data:** We don't analyze, monetize, or share footage. Period.
3. **Bank-Level Encryption:** All cloud data is AES-256 encrypted
4. **Transparent Policy:** No legal jargon—read it at [Link]

**Business Model Clarity:**
We make money selling hardware, NOT subscriptions or data harvesting.

Annual third-party security audits (results published publicly).

Does that address your concern?
```

---

### Scenario 5: FUD / Trolling

**User says:** "This is just another Kickstarter scam."

**Response:**
```
We get it—crowdfunding skepticism is healthy. You should vet every campaign.

**Here's What We Can Point You To:**
- Functional prototypes (video demos in #media-mentions)
- Manufacturing partner with 8-year track record
- Transparent team bios (LinkedIn profiles linked on campaign)
- Refund policy clearly stated in FAQs

If you have specific concerns, we're happy to address them. But if you're 
just here to troll, we'll politely ask you to move along—this community 
is for constructive discussion.

Anyone else have *genuine* questions about credibility? We're an open book.
```

---

### Scenario 6: Feature Request Overload

**User says:** "You NEED to add [Feature X] or I'm not backing."

**Response:**
```
Love the passion! Feature requests are how we get better.

**Here's How We Prioritize:**
1. Drop it in #feature-requests with your use case details
2. Community upvotes (React with 👍)
3. Product team reviews top 10 monthly
4. High-demand features → roadmap

**Reality Check:** We can't promise every feature will make it in 
(engineering tradeoffs are real), but we DO read every request.

[Feature X] specifically: Let's get the community's take. Everyone 
interested in [Feature X], react to [User]'s message with 🔥!
```
