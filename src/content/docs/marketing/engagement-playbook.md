# Engagement Playbook

Ready-to-use templates for viral short-form content and review management. Aligned with the [HeyZack Brand Voice](../brand/voice-tone.md): **The Knowledgeable Friend** (B2C) and **The Helpful Peer** (B2B).

---

## Short-Form Video Hooks

### Platform Overview

| Platform | Optimal Length | Best Practices |
|----------|---------------|----------------|
| TikTok | 15-30s | Text overlay mandatory, 3-5 hashtags |
| Instagram Reels | 15-30s | 10-15 hashtags, sticker engagement |
| YouTube Shorts | 30-60s | Keyword-rich titles, #Shorts required |
| Facebook Reels | 30-60s | Storytelling captions, 5-10 hashtags |

### Posting Schedule

- **TikTok:** 6-9AM, 12-2PM, 7-11PM
- **Instagram Reels:** 9-11AM, 1-3PM, 7-9PM
- **YouTube Shorts:** 12-3PM, 5-9PM
- **Facebook Reels:** 1-4PM, 7-9PM

## Hook Categories

### Category 1: Frustration Validation Hooks

POV videos validating common smart home pain points. Target audience: Tech-Savvy Homeowners, Safety-Focused Families.

#### Hook 1.1: The App Hell Nightmare

**Audio/Text Overlay:** "POV: You have 7 apps just to turn on your lights"

**Visual Action:**  
Frustrated person rapidly swiping through phone apps—Nest, Ring, Philips Hue, SmartThings, Ecobee, August, Arlo—trying to find the right one.

**Script (15s):**
```
(0:00-0:03) POV: You have 7 apps just to turn on your lights.
(0:03-0:07) Nest for climate. Ring for security. Hue for lights. August for locks...
(0:07-0:12) I spent $3,000 on smart home stuff and I'm MORE stressed.
(0:12-0:15) Until I found this. One app. Everything. [Transition to HeyZack app reveal]
```

**Production Notes:**
- Music: Chaotic/frantic beat building tension, then sudden calm
- Hashtags: #SmartHome #TechFail #HomeAutomation #SmartHomeTips
- Target: Tech-Savvy Tyler

---

## Review Response Templates

### Response Strategy by Rating

| Rating | Strategy | Tone |
|--------|----------|------|
| 5 Stars | Gratitude + community invite | Celebratory |
| 4 Stars | Thank + address concern + invite feedback | Constructive |
| 1-3 Stars | Empathy first + resolution + take private | Apologetic |

---

## 5-Star Review Responses

Keep brief (2-3 sentences). Express gratitude, reinforce value, invite community.

### Template 5.1: Energy Savings Callback (B2C)

**Use When:** Customer mentions lower energy bills

```
Hey [Name]! This is exactly why we built HeyZack—energy savings that actually 
show up on your bill. 💚 So glad the [specific feature] is working for you. 
If you know someone still juggling seven different smart home apps, we'd love 
if you'd share your experience!
```

---

### Template 5.2: Simplicity Praise (B2C)

**Use When:** Customer mentions ease of setup

```
[Name], music to our ears! We're obsessed with making smart homes that 
finally 'just work.' Thrilled the [specific feature] delivered for you. 
Welcome to the HeyZack family—check your email for our community invite!
```

---

### Template 5.3: AI Learning Validation (B2C)

**Use When:** Customer mentions the system learning their habits

```
This is the coolest part, [Name]—watching HeyZack learn your unique patterns. 
Sounds like it's nailed your [specific pattern]. Pro tip: the intelligence just 
keeps getting better over the next few months. Mind sharing your story in our 
HeyZack Homes showcase?
```

---

### Template 5.4: Non-Tech Partner Approval (B2C)

**Use When:** Customer mentions spouse/family success

```
[Name], you just passed the ultimate test! 😄 When [partner/mom/dad] can use it 
without calling you for help, that's when we know we got it right. Thanks for 
trusting us to simplify your home!
```

---

### Template 5.5: Portfolio ROI Validation (B2B)

**Use When:** Property manager mentions cost savings

```
[Name], love seeing results like this. Real ROI data is what separates smart 
building hype from actual operational wins. Congrats on the [specific outcome]. 
We'd be honored to feature your case study—interested in sharing your strategy 
with other operators? Email us at success@heyzack.ai.
```

## 4-Star Review Responses

Address concern, invite private conversation, show you're improving.

### Template 4.1: Minor Setup Friction (B2C)

**Use When:** 4 stars with setup confusion mentioned

```
Thanks for the honest feedback, [Name]. Really appreciate you sticking with 
the setup—we know [specific issue] can be tricky in some environments. We're 
updating our setup guides based on feedback like yours. If you hit any other 
snags, DM us or email support@heyzack.ai—we'll walk you through it personally.
```

---

### Template 4.2: Feature Request Implied (B2C/B2B)

**Use When:** "Would be 5 stars if it had..."

```
[Name], thanks for this—constructive feedback is how we get better. Totally 
hear you on [requested feature]. We're evaluating it for a future update, and 
your input just got logged with our product team. Shoot me your email at 
feedback@heyzack.ai if you want to beta test when it's ready!
```

---

### Template 4.3: App UX Suggestion (B2C)

**Use When:** Mentions app could be better

```
Thanks, [Name]. App experience is critical—appreciate you flagging [specific 
issue]. We've got a UX refresh coming in Q[X] and this exact feedback is on 
the list. Would you be open to a 15-minute user testing call? We compensate 
with Amazon gift card. Email product@heyzack.ai if interested!
```

## 1-3 Star Review Responses

Empathy first, defense never. Acknowledge, resolve, take private.

### Template 3.1: Defective Device (1-2 Stars)

**Use When:** Hardware failure

```
[Name], I'm really sorry this happened. This is absolutely not the experience 
we want for you, and we need to make it right immediately.

**Next step:** Please DM me or email priority@heyzack.ai with your order number. 
I'll send a replacement today with expedited shipping at no charge, plus 
[add-on/discount] for the inconvenience.

We stand behind our products—you shouldn't have to deal with this.
```

---

### Template 3.2: Shipping Delay Frustration (2-3 Stars)

**Use When:** Late delivery, shipping issues

```
[Name], I totally understand your frustration. We promised [delivery date] 
and didn't deliver—that's on us, and I'm genuinely sorry.

**What I'm doing:**
1. Tracking shows delivery by [date]—I'll personally monitor
2. Applying [discount/credit] to your account for the delay
3. Flagging this with our logistics team

Email me directly at shipping@heyzack.ai if you need this rushed.
```

---

### Template 3.3: Setup Failure / Technical Issue (2-3 Stars)

**Use When:** Customer can't get it working

```
[Name], really sorry you're hitting this wall. Smart home setup should be 
simple—30 minutes max—and you're clearly not getting that experience.

**Let's troubleshoot:**
1. Check out this video: [troubleshooting link]
2. Schedule a live support call: [calendly link]
3. Or DM me your phone number—I'll call within 2 hours

We don't stop until this works for you. If we can't resolve it, full 
refund—no hassle. Email techsupport@heyzack.ai to jump the queue.
```

---

### Template 3.4: Privacy/Security Concern (2-3 Stars)

**Use When:** Data privacy worries

```
[Name], appreciate you raising this. Privacy and security are non-negotiables.

**Our architecture:**
- Local storage option—footage never leaves your property
- Bank-level encryption (AES-256) for cloud features
- We don't sell data—we sell hardware
- Full data export and deletion available anytime

**Resources:**
- Privacy policy (plain English): [link]
- Security whitepaper: [link]

Email privacy@heyzack.ai—our CTO will respond personally.
```

## Crisis Response Templates

**Use When:** Safety issue, widespread defect

```

safety and product quality extremely seriously.

**What we're doing:**
1. **Immediate:** All affected units being recalled and replaced
2. **Investigation:** Root cause analysis underway
3. **Communication:** All affected customers notified within 24 hours
4. **Resolution timeline:** [Specific date]

**Your next step:**
1. Stop using [affected device] immediately
2. Replacement shipped today with expedited shipping + [compensation]
3. Direct line: crisis@heyzack.ai or [phone number]

We own this 100%. Your safety is non-negotiable.

— [Founder Name], CEO, HeyZack
```
