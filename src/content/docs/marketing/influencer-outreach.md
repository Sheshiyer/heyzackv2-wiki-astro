# Influencer Outreach

Ready-to-use DM scripts and email templates for recruiting influencers and thought leaders. Aligned with the [HeyZack Brand Voice](../brand/voice-tone.md).

---

## Outreach Strategy Overview

| Segment | Platform | Voice | Primary Goal |
|---------|----------|-------|--------------|
| B2B Property Managers | LinkedIn, Email | The Helpful Peer | Partnership + Case Studies |
| B2B Hospitality | LinkedIn, Email | The Helpful Peer | Beta Testing + ROI Proof |
| B2C Tech Reviewers | YouTube, Instagram DM | The Knowledgeable Friend | Honest Reviews |
| B2C Sustainability | Instagram, TikTok | The Knowledgeable Friend | Energy Savings Content |

---

## B2B Influencer Outreach

### Voice Guidelines (B2B)

- **Tone:** Professional, data-driven, peer-to-peer
- **Target Emotions:** Professional Pride, Vendor Liberation, Operational Control
- **Focus:** ROI, efficiency, no vendor lock-in

---

## LinkedIn DM Templates (B2B)

### Template 1: The Peer Recognition Opener

**Use When:** Reaching out to property management thought leaders

```
Hi [Name],

I just read your piece on [specific pain point, e.g., "BMS vendor lock-in" or 
"portfolio energy efficiency"]. You nailed the frustration every property 
manager faces but nobody talks about openly.

I'm [Your Name], part of the team at HeyZack. We've built an AI-powered 
building automation platform that tackles exactly what you wrote about: 
portfolio-scale control at 95% lower cost than traditional BMS systems. 
No service contracts. No vendor lock-in. DIY installation.

We're launching on [Kickstarter/Indiegogo] in [Month] and partnering with 
industry operators like you who actually understand the problem.

Here's what we're offering launch partners:
→ Early access to the platform (test across your properties)
→ Affiliate commission (20% on referrals from your network)
→ Co-branded case study showcasing your portfolio's results

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Title, HeyZack]
[Website Link]
```

```
managers frustrated by the traditional BMS vendor model—$85K installation

I'd like to pitch a guest article for [Publication Name]:

DIY automation to achieve 20-30% energy savings at 95% lower cost than 
legacy BMS systems.

**What Makes This Relevant Now:**
→ Rising energy costs forcing operators to prove efficiency
→ Labor shortages making centralized control critical

I'd include 2-3 operator case studies, transparent cost comparisons, and 
actionable steps for operators considering alternatives.

Would this fit your editorial calendar?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Title, HeyZack]
[Email] | [Phone] | [Website Link]
```

## Follow-Up Cadence

### No Response After Initial Outreach

**Day 3-4:** Light bump
```
Hey [Name], just bubbling this up in case it got buried. No pressure—
let me know either way!
```

**Day 7-10:** Value-add follow-up
```
Hi [Name], wanted to share this: [relevant article/case study/video]. 
Thought you'd find it interesting given your content on [topic]. 
Still happy to send a review unit if you're interested.
```

**Day 14:** Final attempt
```
Last ping, promise! If timing isn't right, totally understand. 
We'll be around—feel free to reach out whenever.
```
