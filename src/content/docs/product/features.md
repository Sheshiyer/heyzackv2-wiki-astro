# Features & Benefits

This document provides a comprehensive breakdown of HeyZack's platform features, organized by capability area with clear customer benefits for both residential and commercial users.

## Core Platform Features

### Unified Control App

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Technical Spec** | Single iOS/Android app for all 128 product types |
| **Customer Benefit** | No more juggling 7 different apps |
| **Why It Matters** | Reduces cognitive load, saves time, eliminates app-switching frustration |

### Genuine AI Learning

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Technical Spec** | Behavioral pattern recognition (not rule-based) |
| **Customer Benefit** | Learns habits automatically—no programming required |
| **Why It Matters** | Automation improves over time without manual effort |

The AI system observes actual usage patterns and makes proactive adjustments. Unlike competitors who market "AI" but deliver timer-based automation, HeyZack's pattern recognition delivers measurable improvements: energy savings increase from 15% → 20% → 28% over the first 6 months.

### Local + Cloud Hybrid Architecture

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Technical Spec** | Edge computing + optional cloud features |

## Use Case Highlights

### Morning Routine (B2C)
Tyler never programmed a routine. HeyZack learned: weekdays at 6:45 AM, motion in bedroom triggers gradual light increase to 40%, coffee maker start, and thermostat adjustment to 70°F. On weekends, the routine shifts to 8:30 AM automatically.

### Energy Discovery (B2C)
Tyler's bill jumped $40. HeyZack energy dashboard identified: "Basement refrigerator consuming 3.2x normal." Problem: old beer fridge door seal failing. $30 fix resulted in $35/month savings.

### Remote Climate Control (B2B)
Patricia manages 30 buildings. Tenant calls from Building F about temperature. She opens the app, sees thermostat at 78°F, remotely adjusts to 74°F, confirms with tenant—all while in a different city. Resolution time: 90 seconds.

### Elder Care Monitoring (B2C)
Sarah's mom (73) lives with the family. HeyZack tracks morning routine: typically bathroom motion 7-8 AM. One morning, no motion by 9:30 AM triggers alert. System catches routine deviations that could indicate emergencies.
